How to use this page
Expiration dates are derived from the deal year and duration in the benchmark database. Where duration is unknown, deals are marked as such. Use this page to identify competitor renewal windows — when a rival club's jersey becomes available, or when a brand may be looking for a new basketball partnership. Deals marked Renews now are either expired or expiring within 12 months.
