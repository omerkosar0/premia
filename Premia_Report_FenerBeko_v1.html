Fenerbahçe Beko · Strategic Recommendations
2025–26 EuroLeague
Key findings
Finding 01
The Beko naming deal is priced below fair market value
The jersey front asset generates €10.94M in annual media value based on verified audience data and CPM benchmarking. The estimated current deal at ~€4M/year represents 37% of verified value — below the 40–60% fair market range used across European sports. At renewal, the data supports a counter-offer of €4.4M–€6.6M with full methodology documentation.
Finding 02
The Adidas kit deal is commercially aligned
The Adidas kit manufacturer deal at €10M/year (confirmed, 2025) covers the kit manufacturer asset which delivers €5.34M in broadcast media value annually. The deal is a broader commercial partnership that also includes design exclusivity, retail revenue share (65%), and amateur sports funding — making it appropriately valued when all components are considered.
Finding 03
LED and in-venue assets are significantly underpriced as a package
LED perimeter boards, shot clock, and coach kit together deliver €4.96M in annual media value. These assets are typically bundled and sold at a discount as secondary sponsorships. Based on benchmark data, there is a strong case for unbundling and pricing the LED slot independently at €600K–€900K/year per sponsor rotation.
Recommendations
1
Beko renewal: Initiate renewal negotiation with independent valuation data. Counter-offer range: €4.4M–€5.5M/year. Present Premia's methodology as third-party verification. Use the Fenerbahçe football Chobani deal (€4M for European-only jersey) as the floor anchor.
2
LED board unbundling: Separate LED rotation from bundled secondary packages. Price each rotation slot at €600K–€900K/year based on verified media value. At 6 rotating sponsors per game, annual LED revenue potential: €2.4M–€3.6M.
3
Press backdrop activation: The press backdrop has the highest quality index (1.0) of any asset — clean, unobstructed, camera-facing. Currently under-commercialised. Target brands in consumer electronics, beverages, or financial services seeking premium media quality. Benchmark pricing: €400K–€700K/year.
4
Social media formalisation: Current social media sponsor integrations deliver €456K/year in media value but are typically offered as add-ons rather than priced assets. Formalise social packages at €180K–€300K/year per primary sponsor as a standalone product.
